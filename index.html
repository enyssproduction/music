Informations

Hello! I am Enyss, a dedicated composer & sound designer who handles sound design, sfx and original soundtracks for video-games/movies.
My soundtracks are in various styles such as fantasy, horror, liminal, synthwave, nintendo-like, ambiant...

My first and still continuing today collaboration is with the content creator "Leaf ." on YouTube for his story-telling videos about horror & video-games such as Minecraft, Five Nights at Freddy's and more.





I've been producing for around 5 years on the DAW "FL Studio" & "MPC Beats" with the help of 2 synthetizors from MPC Akai Professional.
I produce for a few games that you can find in "Experiences" and movies in "Movies". My composing process begins with a deep understanding of the game’s narrative, themes, and emotions. I believe that music should not only only complement the gameplay but also elevate the storytelling and make each moment memorable. I work closely with developers to ensure that my soundtracks fit perfectly with the narration of the game.
I can also provide the publication of your game soundtracks on streaming plateforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube Music, Deezer, Tiktok, Instagram and much more !
