Informations

Hello! I am Enyss, a dedicated composer and sound designer specializing in creating original soundtracks for video games.
My soundtracks are mostly horror/liminal/synthwave themed, but I also produce different other styles such as "epic" and fantasy-sounding music.

My first and still continuing today collaboration is with the content creator "Leaf ." on YouTube for his videos about horror stories and video-games like Minecraft, Five Nights at Freddy's, and more.





I've been producing for around 5 years on the DAW "FL Studio" & "MPC Beats" with the help of 2 synthetizors from MPC Akai Professional.
I produce for a few games that you can find in the "Experiences" category. My composing process begins with a deep understanding of the game’s narrative, themes, and emotions. I believe that music should not only only complement the gameplay but also elevate the storytelling and make each moment memorable. I work closely with developers to ensure that my soundtracks fit perfectly with the narration of the game.
My prices are very low (if not free) and I also provide pulication of your game soundtracks on streaming plateforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube Music, Deezer, Tiktok, Instagram and much more !
